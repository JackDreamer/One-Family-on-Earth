# One Family on Earth | 地球一家人

互联网全球互助社区 | Global Internet Mutual Aid Community
**本项目所有内容仅代表参与者个人观点，与任何企业、机构、组织均无关联。**

## 项目宗旨 | Project Purpose
世界本就是一个开放的世界，我们来自不同国家、不同行业、不同阶段，却都在为生活、理想与未来努力奋斗。
本项目旨在搭建一个无国界的互助平台，让每一位奋斗者都能在这里提出困惑、分享经验、彼此支撑。

The world is inherently an open place. We come from different countries, industries and life stages, yet we all strive for life, dreams and futures.
This project aims to build a borderless mutual aid platform, where every struggler can raise confusion, share experience and support each other.

## 核心功能 | Core Functions
- 在对应文件中提出你的问题，不限主题、不限字数
- 其他参与者可在下方直接作答，提供建议与帮助
- 内容统一使用**中文 + 英文**呈现
- 其他语言需同步附上中文与英文翻译
- 纯粹互助，无广告、无攻击、无商业行为

- Raise your questions in corresponding files, no topic or word limit
- Other participants can reply directly below with advice and help
- All content shall be presented in **Chinese + English**
- Other languages must be accompanied by Chinese and English translations
- Pure mutual aid, no advertisements, attacks or commercial activities

## 共建愿景 | Vision for Collaboration
不分地域，不分语言，不分背景。
愿我们在这个开放的互联网里，真正成为**地球一家人**。

Regardless of region, language or background.
May we truly become **One Family on Earth** in this open Internet.


项目初衷 | About the Author & Initiative
我是本项目的发起者，一名来自中国的 32 岁 Android 语音客户端开发工程师。创建「地球一家人」这个互助空间，初衷很简单：我希望能听到来自世界各地优秀朋友的真实想法与人生经验，为自己迷茫的阶段找到一些方向与力量；同时也希望用这份小小的善意，去陪伴和帮助更多在路上的人。
我们都活在一个充满焦虑与不安的世界，战乱与动荡从未离我们很远。我发自内心地期盼世界和平，愿每一个人都能在安稳中生活，在善意里成长。我非常渴望能在这里和大家一起学习、彼此交流、共同进步。
我当下真实的人生难题是：我很想提升自己、改变现状，却常常不知道从何处开始、往哪里走，正处在一段迷茫又无助的阶段。也期待在这里，和大家一起找到属于自己的答案。
I am the creator of this project, a 32-year-old Android voice client developer from China.My simple wish for building One Family on Earth is to hear genuine thoughts and life experiences from outstanding people around the world, so I can find direction and strength for myself in this confusing period of life. Meanwhile, I hope to use this small act of kindness to accompany and help more people who are on the same journey.
We all live in a world full of anxiety and uncertainty. Wars and turmoils are never far away. I sincerely wish for world peace, hoping that everyone can live in safety and grow with kindness.I am eager to learn, exchange ideas and grow together with all of you here.
The real dilemma I am facing right now is: I desperately want to improve myself and change my current situation, but I often don’t know where to start or where to go. I am in a lost and helpless stage of life. I also hope that, together with all of you here, we can find our own answers.
