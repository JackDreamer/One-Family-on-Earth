# Contributing Guide | 贡献指南

欢迎加入 One Family on Earth 地球一家人互助计划，以下是简单的参与规则：

Welcome to the One Family on Earth Mutual Aid Project. Here are the simple rules for participation:

---

## 一、如何提问 | How to Ask Questions
1. 在 `questions/` 目录下新建文件，格式：`question-序号.md`
2. 标题统一格式：`Question X: 中文标题 | English Title`
3. 问题内容必须同时使用**中文 + 英文**书写
4. 问题真诚、清晰、具体，便于他人理解与回答

1. Create a new file in the `questions/` directory, format: `question-number.md`
2. Unified title format: `Question X: Chinese Title | English Title`
3. Questions must be written in **both Chinese and English**
4. Questions should be sincere, clear and specific for others to understand and answer

---

## 二、如何回答 | How to Answer
1. 在对应问题文件的「回答区 | Answers」下方续写内容
2. 回答需同时提供**中文 + 英文**版本
3. 字数不限，态度友善、理性、真诚
4. 可以分享经验、提供思路、给予鼓励，禁止嘲讽、攻击、引战

1. Add content under the "Answers" section of the corresponding question file
2. Answers must be provided in **both Chinese and English**
3. No word limit, be friendly, rational and sincere
4. You can share experience, provide ideas or give encouragement; satire, attacks and conflicts are prohibited

---

## 三、语言规范 | Language Rules
- 优先使用：中文 / 英文
- 其他语言必须附上**中文 + 英文**完整翻译
- 内容文明、理性、正向，拒绝歧视与极端言论

- Priority languages: Chinese / English
- Other languages must be accompanied by complete **Chinese + English** translations
- Content should be civilized, rational and positive; discrimination and extreme remarks are prohibited

---

## 四、内容红线 | Content Red Lines
- 不涉及政治、色情、暴力、违法信息
- 不发布广告、引流、传销、理财诱导等内容
- 不泄露他人隐私，不进行人身攻击
- 不传播谣言、不实信息与极端观点

- No politics, pornography, violence or illegal information
- No advertisements, traffic guidance, pyramid schemes or financial induction
- No disclosure of others' privacy or personal attacks
- No spread of rumors, false information or extreme views
