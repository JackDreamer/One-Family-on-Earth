# | If you were to start a business, what would you do?

## 提问 | Question
如果抛开当下的工作、现实的压力，
只跟着自己的内心和认知去选择一次，
如果让你去创业，你最想做的事情是什么？

是做一个产品，一项服务，还是一个平台？
你希望它能解决什么样的问题，又能给这个世界带来些什么？

Putting aside your current job and real-life pressures,
if you could follow your heart and choose just once,
what would you most want to do if you started a business?

Would it be a product, a service, or a platform?
What problem do you hope it solves, and what value would it bring to the world?
